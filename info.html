You Are Not Alone

Mental health struggles affect millions of people. No matter what you’re going through, support and understanding exist.

Common Mental Health Disorders

Anxiety

Anxiety can cause excessive worry, fear, and physical symptoms like a racing heart or shortness of breath.

Depression

Depression affects mood, motivation, and energy, making everyday activities feel overwhelming.

PTSD

PTSD may develop after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event, causing flashbacks or emotional distress.

Stress & Burnout

Long-term stress can lead to emotional exhaustion, anxiety, and physical health problems.

Stories & Support Communities

Our Stories

"When I was a kid, I always hated crowded places. It felt like everywhere I went, there were eyes on me. As I got older, just being around large groups of people even family made me anxious. It got to the point where I wouldn't want to go anywhere. But I was tired of missing out on hanging out with my friends, so I decided to get counseling. That’s when I learned I have social anxiety, and that so many other people deal with it too. Knowing this made me feel less alone. My counselor also gave me tools to help me handle being in crowded areas, which has made a huge difference.”

— Mack H -Site Creator
